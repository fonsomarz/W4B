About Me

As a child I never really knew what I wanted to do. I just knew that I loved to learn. Taking things apart just to see what was inside, not really knowing how to put them back together but eventually figuring it out and leaving it the way I found it. Curiosity is an amazing thing that can lead you down so many roads and adventures. If it wasn't for that I would not be where I am today. For the past 10 years I have been teaching myself everything that I can about filmmaking, photography, and editing. There is still so much to learn. I hope I will learn from more experience, skills and continue improving my talents. Learning is a passion of mine,and I love teaching all that I know to those who come next. I am Married to my best friend; my beautiful wife Elsie. We have a small family of two cats, Mimi and Loki.
